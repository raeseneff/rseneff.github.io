Favorite Recipe: Beef Kilbasa Stir Fry

Ingredients!!

  • One white onion
  • 2 Bell Peppers (does not matter color but personally use green bell peppers)
  • Beef kilbasa
  • Olive oil of your choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps to cook!!

  1. Cut up onions and peppers into strips
  2. Cut the Kilbasa into rounds
  3. Put olive oil into a skillet
  4. Put in the onions and season with salt and pepper
  5. Cook onions for five minutes and then add in the peppers
  6. Once the peppers have started to soften, put in the kilbasa and cook till it becomes a brown to your liking.
  7. Make sure to taste it before fully finishing
  8. Add salt and pepper if needed
